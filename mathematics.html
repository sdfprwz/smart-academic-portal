📘 About the Department
Department Overview
The Department of Mathematics at Central University of South Bihar is dedicated to fostering mathematical excellence through quality education, advanced research, and innovation. We offer comprehensive programs spanning undergraduate to doctoral levels, with emphasis on analytical thinking, problem-solving, and mathematical rigor.
Vision & Mission
Vision: To be a leading department in mathematical sciences producing highly trained mathematicians and researchers.

Mission: To impart quality education aligned with CUSB's motto "ज्ञान सेवा विमुक्तये" (Knowledge, Service, Liberation), conduct impactful research, and foster innovation for societal benefit.
