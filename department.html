📘 About the Department
Department Overview
The Department of Computer Science at Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) is dedicated to advancing the field of computer science through quality education, cutting-edge research, and fostering innovation. Established as part of CUSB's commitment to excellence in higher education, the department offers comprehensive programs in Computer Science and related disciplines.
Vision & Mission
Vision: To be a leading department producing globally competitive computer scientists and professionals.

Mission: To impart quality education aligned with CUSB's motto "ज्ञान सेवा विमुक्तये" (Knowledge, Service, Liberation), conduct impactful research, and foster innovation for societal benefit.
